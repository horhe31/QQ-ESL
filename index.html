(JP) Rapport Building


  • What's your name? お名前は？

  • How are you today? ご機嫌いかがですか？

  • What do you do? どうするんだ？

  • What did you do today? 今日は何をしましたか？

  • What are your plans for today? 今日の予定は？

  • Can you tell me about some of your interests and hobbies?
    興味や趣味について教えてください。

  • If you could live anywhere, where would it be?
    どこにでも住めるとしたら、どこに住みたいですか？

  • How long have you been studying English?
    いつから英語を勉強していますか？

  • Why do you study English? なぜ英語を勉強するのですか？

  • Have you taken any English tests or lessons before?
    以前に英語のテストやレッスンを受けたことがありますか？

Japanese Intructions


  • Can you hear me? 聞こえますか？

  • Can you see me? 私が見えますか？

  • Check the chat box. チャットボックスにチェックを入れる。

  • Please listen and repeat. 繰り返し聞いてください。

  • Please repeat after me. 私の後にもう一度言ってください。

  • Please listen. お聞きください。

  • Please read. お読みください。

  • Please draw a circle. 円を描いてください。

  • Do you have any questions? 何か質問はありますか？

  • Please answer the question. 質問に答えてください。

  • Let's switch roles and ask me the questions.
    役割を交代して、私に質問しよう。

(CN) Rapport Building


  • What's your name? 你叫什么名字？

  • How are you today? 你今天怎么样？

  • What do you do? 你做什么工作？

  • What did you do today? 你今天做了什么？

  • What are your plans for today? 您今天有什么计划？

  • Can you tell me about some of your interests and hobbies?
    你能告诉我一些你的兴趣和爱好吗？

  • If you could live anywhere, where would it be?
    如果你可以住在任何地方，你会选择哪里？

  • How long have you been studying English?

  • Why do you study English? 你为什么学习英语？

  • Have you taken any English tests or lessons before?

Sample Free-Conversation Questions


  • If you were a superhero, what would be your name and special powers be?
  • Would you rather play sports or watch them?
  • What do you hope to be like in 10 years?
  • What was the hardest thing you’ve ever done?
  • If you had only three wishes, what would they be?
  • If you could go back in time, what advice would you give yourself?
  • What is a big dream or goal you have for the future?
  • What activities do you love doing after school/work?
  • What places have you traveled to before? Which was your favorite?
  • What is your favorite movie or show? Tell me about it.
  • What is something you used to be scared of but aren’t anymore?
  • What was the best gift you’ve ever received? Tell me about it.
  • What is your favorite holiday? Why?
  • Have you ever sung in public?
  • Have you ever played an instrument in public?
  • If you had a time machine, would you rather go back in time or travel to the future?
  • If you won a million dollars, what would you do with the money? Why?
  • Would you rather live in the country or the city?

Assessment Tagging


    Student is ABSENT


    Please paste these comments in the teacher's box ONLY.

  • - The student didn't enter the classroom/zoom.
  • - I sent the reminder by clicking the "reminder button" within 5 mins.
  • - I waited until the class ended, but still, the student didn't enter.
  • - So, I tagged the lesson as INCOMPLETE and chose STUDENT ABSENT.
  • - Please refer to the previous lesson. Thank you!

    Message to the student

  • Hello, you missed our class today, so I marked it as incomplete. However, I'm really excited to see you, so please consider having another class, and we will have a great and interesting session together. Have a wonderful day until we meet next!

  • (JP TRANSLATION)
    こんにちは、今日の授業をお休みされたので、未完了としました。でも、あなたに会えるのをとても楽しみにしています。またお会いできる日を楽しみにしています！
  • (CN TRANSLATION)
    你好，你错过了我们今天的课程，所以我标记为未完成。不过，我真的很高兴见到你，所以请你考虑再上一次课，我们会一起度过一个愉快而有趣的一天。祝你愉快，下次再见！


    INFORMED Absence Notice from the Student


    Please paste these comments in the teacher's box ONLY.

  • - The student sent an informed absence notice
  • - So, I tagged the lesson as INCOMPLETE and chose OTHERS.
  • - Please refer to the previous lesson. Thank you!

    Message to the student

  • Hello, you missed our class today, so I marked it as incomplete. However, I'm really excited to see you, so please consider having another class, and we will have a great and interesting session together. Have a wonderful day until we meet next!



    Class lasted for less than 20/40 minutes


    Please paste these comments above the teacher's note

  • Note: The student left the classroom and did not enter again. Please follow the prevoius assessment.

    Message to the student

  • Hello, I tagged the lesson as INCOMPLTE due to time constraints. I'm looking forward to seeing you next time!


    Technical Problem / Teacher's late / No Electricity


    Please paste these comments above the teacher's note

  • Note: The teacher is late entering the classroom due to (Internet Connection Problem)
  • -So, I tagged the lesson as INCOMPLETE and chose (INTERNET CONNECTION PROBLEM)
  • -Please refer to the previous lesson. Thank you!

    Message to the student

  • Hello, I tagged the lesson as INCOMPLTE due to time constraints. I'm looking forward to seeing you next time!

OPTIONAL: Motivational Messages to the student

  • You are doing an incredible job with your English studies! I can see your progress every day. Keep up the excellent work.

  • Don't be discouraged by challenges along the way. I believe in your ability to overcome any obstacles. See you next time!

  • I can see a lot of improvements with your English skills (student's name). Keep up the good work and see you next time!
